Admission Criteria & Eligibility

General eligibility for all BS programs: Candidates must have passed Intermediate (FA/FSc/ICS) or equivalent from a recognized board with a minimum of 45% marks (as per University of the Punjab BS admission rules). Admissions are granted on merit. Applicants with O/A Levels must provide an equivalence certificate.
