About Us

F.G Post Graduate College is committed to providing quality education and fostering excellence in learning. With experienced faculty and modern facilities, we prepare students for successful careers and meaningful lives. Our mission is to create a supportive learning environment where every student can reach their full potential.

College Overview

F.G Post Graduate College for Women, Wah Cantt

Established to empower women through higher education, the college offers BS programs across sciences, social sciences and humanities. Located in Wah Cantt, the campus provides a safe, inclusive environment with modern classrooms, computer labs, a well-stocked library, and active student societies. Academic excellence is supported by experienced faculty, merit-based admissions and continuous mentoring.

  • Affiliation: University of the Punjab (BS Programs)
  • Facilities: Library, IT Labs, Science Labs, Sports, Cafeteria
  • Focus: Character building, research skills and community engagement

Our Programs

Admission Criteria & Eligibility

General eligibility for all BS programs: Candidates must have passed Intermediate (FA/FSc/ICS) or equivalent from a recognized board with a minimum of 45% marks (as per University of the Punjab BS admission rules). Admissions are granted on merit. Applicants with O/A Levels must provide an equivalence certificate.

About University of the Punjab

University of the Punjab (PU) Lahore is one of Pakistan’s oldest and most prestigious public universities. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, strong research culture, and vibrant campus life. F.G Post Graduate College for Women, Wah Cantt operates BS programs affiliated with PU, following PU’s academic standards.

  • Public University with extensive faculties and departments
  • Quality assurance, examinations and curriculum oversight
  • Strong alumni network and industry linkages
